Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and products

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers. For enterprise solutions, we also offer invoice-based payments.

How long does shipping take?

+

Shipping times vary by location: Domestic orders typically arrive within 2-4 business days, while international shipping can take 7-14 business days. Express shipping options are available.

What is your return policy?

+

We offer a 30-day return policy for unused items in their original packaging. Custom orders and opened software packages are non-refundable. Contact our support team to initiate a return.

Do you offer technical support?

+

Yes, we provide 24/7 technical support for all our products. Support is available via email, phone, and live chat. Enterprise customers receive priority support with dedicated account managers.
